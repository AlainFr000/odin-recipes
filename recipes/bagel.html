Salmon bagel

Description

These yummy pickle bacon chopped bagels are made with everything bagels, that are toasted and filled with a quick chopped cream cheese, bacon, and dill pickle spread.

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces softened cream cheese
  • 1/3 cup dill pickle slices
  • 2 teaspoons pickle brine from the jar
  • 2 green onions, cut into 3-inch pieces
  • 4 slices cooked bacon
  • 4 dill sprigs
  • 2 everything bagels, sliced in half and toasted

Steps

  1. Place cream cheese onto a cutting board. Top cream cheese with pickles, brine, bacon, green onions, and dill. Chop all of the ingredients together and mix until well combined.

  2. Divide mixture onto the bottom halves of bagels; place top halves on. Cut in half and serve immediately.
